Academic Programs

Upper Primary Education (Classes VI-VIII)

The upper primary section builds on foundational knowledge with more specialized subjects and introduction to science and social studies.

Curriculum:

  • First Language: Bengali
  • Second Language: English
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • History
  • Geography
  • Computer Education

Secondary Education (Classes IX-X)

Secondary education prepares students for the Madhyamik Pariksha (Class X Board Examination) conducted by WBBSE.

Curriculum (As per WBBSE):

  • First Language: Bengali
  • Second Language: English
  • Mathematics
  • Physical Science
  • Life Science
  • History
  • Geography
  • Optional Subject (Choose one):
    • Computer Application
    • Economics
    • Work Education
