About Our School

Learn about our history, mission, and facilities

Our History

Lowgram High School, Lowgram was established in 2008 with the vision to provide quality education to the children of rural West Bengal. Initially starting with just 5 teachers and 45 students, we have grown to become one of the premier educational institutions in the district.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accessible, quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values to become responsible citizens. We strive to create an environment that fosters intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and social responsibility.
